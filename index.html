Home Titan

Titan

Name:Titan
ID:D2027
**Note: Adoption in Northern California - SF Bay Area Only**
Age: 2 years
Sex: male
Breed: Rat Terrier mix
Date Posted: 7/17/07

Description: We rescued Titan from a shelter (Alameda) where he was running out of time. At the shelter, he was dog friendly. But once in a foster home, he started to become dog-aggressive to new dogs and had redirect behavioral problem. Very friendly with people. But there's something special about Titan so we decided to put in an extra effort to help him and is responding very well with dogs on-site now at a training facility. At the facility, he now avoids trouble with other dogs, but still need supervision. He's a beautiful dog, very affectionate and loves people. Loves to play ball and well behaved at facility. He needs a very experienced german shepherd person with leadership who will continue his training and exercise him daily. His trainers are willing to continue working with whomever adopts him. He now has a bright future and will thank you every day.

Home Tad

Tad

Name:Tad
ID:D2343
**Note: Adoption in Northern California - SF Bay Area Only**
Age: 7 years 5 months
Sex: male
Breed: Miniature Pinscher Mix
Date Posted: 3/4/09

Description:He was adopted in June, 2009, but was returned because his adoptive home could not give him the attention he deserved. On 11/1/12, he had to have most of his tail amputated due to an accident with a car door, but he is recovered now. Tad is smart, friendly, curious, loyal, and he lives up to the Miniature Pinscher’s reputation as the “king of toys”. He is nervous of change but settles to a routine. He would be happy lying next to you on the couch or proudly prancing in front of you on a walk. Like all dogs, he needs daily exercise but is not a high energy dog. He loves treats, is eager to learn new tricks and is quite the jumper. Tad was transferred to a new foster home in April, 2013, where he is being called Dobie. The new foster home reports that Dobie adapts to new situations with ease. He is polite, playful, comfortable in a crate, goes potty outside, and is a good alarm barker. He has learned a few new tricks, and his foster is working on his anxiety issues while riding in a car. Dobie would like to be your new best friend in a quiet adult home.

Home Stella

Stella

Name:Stella
ID:D2740
**Note: Adoption in Northern California - SF Bay Area Only**
Age: Cairn Terrier / Yorkshire Terrier Mix
Sex: female
Breed:11 years 10 months
Date Posted:11/8/11

Description:Stella was rescued on 11/7/11 from a crowded shelter (Alameda) where she was out of time. She is a middle age sr. sweetie. Good with other dogs. No cats (she will bark & try to chase). She was badly matted & dirty at the shelter. Update from foster home: She's a silly, playful fun dog who loves to go for walks, and is great with other dogs. She tries to be the alpha, so needs leadership. She may guard person or home if allowed to. She also loves laps and gives lots of kisses. Kids 12+. Her activity level is moderate. Loves to play with toys, but not hyper in anyway. She does try to climb up on anything she can get to- desks, tables, etc. and she can jump very well. Hard to believe she is 10 yrs young. We will get a 2nd vet to age her. She looks about 8 yrs to us. She is very curious and spunky at home. Look how cute she is after her grooming. In dog years, she is a healthy 56 years old in human years.
